Get Inspired and Disciplined

Begin your journey by finding inspiration and developing the discipline needed to see your project through to completion. Nurture your creative mindset while establishing structured approaches to innovation.

Problem Exploring

Investigate the problem space by observing, questioning, and researching. Uncover the underlying issues and opportunities without rushing to solutions. Dive deep into understanding what truly matters.

Problem Framing

Articulate your challenge in a clear, actionable way. Frame the problem to reveal new perspectives and establish a solid foundation for innovative solutions. The right problem statement opens doors to creativity.

User Research

Understand your users deeply through interviews, observations, and empathy. Learn their needs, desires, and pain points to ground your innovations in real human experiences. Let their stories guide your process.

Ideation

Generate a wide range of potential solutions through brainstorming and creative thinking techniques. Focus on quantity over quality initially, allowing wild ideas to emerge before filtering for feasibility.

Prototyping

Create tangible representations of your ideas quickly and cheaply. Build to think, transforming abstract concepts into something you can test. Low-fidelity prototypes help visualize potential solutions early.

Testing

Put your prototypes in front of real users to gather genuine feedback. Observe what works, what doesn't, and why. Create a safe space for honest critique that will strengthen your solution.

Prototyping (Iterate)

Refine your solution based on testing insights. Embrace an iterative mindset, understanding that each cycle brings you closer to an optimal outcome. Continuously improve through cycles of making and testing.

MVP

Develop your Minimum Viable Product with just enough features to satisfy early users. Focus on core functionality that delivers value while minimizing resources spent. Create something that can be shipped and validated.

Business Model Development

Design a viable, sustainable business model around your solution. Define how your innovation will create, deliver, and capture value. Ensure your great idea has a path to financial sustainability.

Go to Market

Launch your solution and introduce it to the world. Develop strategies for reaching your target users and customers. Craft compelling messaging that resonates with your audience's needs and aspirations.

Growth Strategy

Plan for expansion and scaling your solution. Identify opportunities to reach more users, enhance offerings, and develop your innovation's full potential. Balance short-term wins with long-term vision.

Demo Day

Present your solution to stakeholders, investors, or the wider community. Craft a compelling narrative about your journey and the value your innovation brings. Showcase both the solution and the thinking behind it.

Feedback Collection, Reflect and Connect the Dots

Gather comprehensive feedback, reflect on your entire journey, and identify connections between stages. Analyze successes and failures to extract valuable lessons. Prepare to begin a new cycle of innovation with deeper wisdom.
